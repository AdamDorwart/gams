Detailed Description

Represents a rotation or orientation within a reference frame.

Uses axis-angle notation: a rotation is represented by a vector whose direction forms the axis of rotation, with angle of rotation equal to length of the vector.

All rotations about an axis follow the right hand role; if the origin is the center of your right hand, and your thumb is pointing in the positive direction of the rotation axis, rotations curve in the direction your fingers are pointing.
