Formation flying has several parameters.

Parameters
head_idtarget of the formation
offsetoffset of formation
destinationdestination of the formation
membersnumber of members
modifiermodifier that influences the formation
knowledgethe context containing variables and values
platformthe underlying platform the algorithm will use
sensorsmap of sensor names to sensor information
selfself-referencing variables

The head of the formation is what the other agents key off of to determine their location. Offset is the agent's specified location (in cylindrical coordinates) relative to the head agent. Destination is the final position for the head agent. Members is the number of members in the formation, used to synchronize starting. Modifier is either NONE or ROTATE (rotate the formation).

These values are used because they were found to produce simulations that looked good in VREP. These will be parameterized when we perform real world experiments, but for now will remain hardcoded.
